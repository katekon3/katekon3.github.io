What is it?

Trio is a cards game that involves memory and manipulation. The objective is to have the fewest points at the end, while players will try to get rid of high value cards.

Project goals

  • Design a fun card game that will improve memory and strategic thinking.
  • Make it simple for play and suitable for all ages from teenagers to veterans.

    • How to play

    1. The game starts with shuffling the deck, giving each player 4 cards facing down.

    2. Each player picks a card from the stockpile or the discard pile and then may:

    a. Keep the card and place one of his cards in the discard pile.

    b. Discard it.

    3. Call "Trio" and the player with the lowest score wins.
